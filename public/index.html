About Cache-ual Corner

Cache-ual Corner a persistant chatroom. Intentionally removing modern chatroom aesthetic to intentionally give the nostalgic feeling of what chatrooms 'used to be'.

Cache-ual Corner is an app that is coded in a css file, and then a 'skin' css file is overlayed on top. Users have the ability to go into their personal settings and change their avatars and font color, to what 'skin' they would like to be displayed to design a unique experience to their chat session.

Users and chat messages are stored in a persistant database using JawsDB.

Developers: Vivian Tuong Nguyen (back-end lead) | Will McCrory(front-end lead) | Priya Ammaiyappan (back-end) | Dennis Markham (front-end) | Sergey Sambros(Tester)
